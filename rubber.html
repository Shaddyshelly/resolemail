Natural & Synthetic Rubber

High-performance elastomers for industrial and commercial applications

SBR (Styrene Butadiene Rubber)

Widely used for its abrasion resistance and aging stability. Common in tires, footwear,
and conveyor belts, SBR balances performance and cost for general-purpose applications.

>
PBR (Polybutadiene Rubber)

Offers high resilience and low hysteresis. Ideal for tires and impact resistance. Enhances
fuel efficiency and wear resistance in automotive parts.

>
NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber)

Excellent resistance to oils and chemicals. Used in automotive gaskets, hoses, seals, gloves,
and industrial belting. Performs across various temperatures.

>
SSBR (Solution Styrene Butadiene Rubber)

Used in high-performance tires. Offers excellent grip, rolling resistance, and wear. Boosts
fuel economy and wet traction in automotive uses.

>
EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer)

Excellent resistance to weather, ozone, and heat. Common in roofing, automotive weather seals,
and industrial gaskets. Great for outdoor use.

>
Butyl Rubber

Highly impermeable to air. Used in tire inner liners, stoppers, protective clothing. Excellent
chemical resistance and sealing performance.

>
Chlorobutyl Rubber

Improved aging and curing properties over regular butyl. Ideal for tire sidewalls, hoses, and
pharma closures due to low permeability and stability.

>
Isoprene Rubber (IR)

Synthetic version of natural rubber. Excellent elasticity and strength. Common in medical
products, baby care items, and shock-absorbing uses.

>
CSM Rubber (Chlorosulfonated Polyethylene)

Highly resistant to weathering, ozone, and chemicals. Suitable for industrial hoses,
membranes, and coatings exposed to harsh conditions.

>
Silicon Rubber

Offers flexibility and heat resistance. Non-reactive and biocompatible. Used in cookware,
electronics, medical, and sealing applications.

>
SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene)

Thermoplastic elastomer for adhesives, footwear, and modified asphalt. Combines rubber-like
elasticity with thermoplastic ease of processing.

>
SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene)

Soft, durable elastomer used in toys, healthcare, automotive, and hygiene products. Resistant
to UV and oxidation, ideal for demanding uses.
