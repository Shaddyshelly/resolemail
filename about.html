Who We Are

Delivering Excellence in Polymer Distribution Since 2005

About Resol Vinyl and Chlorides Ltd. (RVCL)

We, Resol Vinyls and Chlorides Ltd. (RVCL) are your reliable partner for high-quality polymer products distribution. RVCL established in 2005 by Mr. Krishan Kumar Bansal as Salasar Impex Pvt. Ltd. Our company has undergone significant growth, transitioning to a Limited company in June 2018 and officially becoming Resol Vinyls and Chlorides Ltd. (RVCL) in March 2022. Our leadership team, including Mr. Krishan Kumar Bansal, Mr. Parth Dodeja who joined in 2016 and Mr. Vijay Rawal who joined in 2021 (however associated since August 2019) brings a shared commitment to growth, innovation, and ensuring our customers succeed.

RVCL is a premier importer and distributor of a wide range of polymers, including PVC Resin, Paste Resin, Melamine, Citric Acid, Polyethylene, Plasticizers and more. Built on a strong foundation of integrity and a deep commitment to our customers, we have become a trusted partner across various industries by consistently providing excellent value and service. As first-generation entrepreneurs, our founders infuse RVCL with fresh energy and a forward-thinking approach that continues to drive our progress.

Our Journey: From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

2005 – The Beginning:
Our story began in 2005 with a clear vision and a sharp focus on chemicals and PVC resin. Through determination, strategic planning, and a commitment to excellence, we emerged as one of the leading importers of PVC resin in India within just five years.

Consistent Year-on-Year Growth:
Driven by an experienced and dedicated team, we have steadily expanded our product portfolio and strengthened our market presence. Our consistent year-on-year growth reflects the trust our clients place in us and our relentless pursuit of innovation and quality.

Nationwide Reach:
Today, our operations extend across the entire country. With a robust PAN India network, we efficiently serve a wide spectrum of industries, delivering reliable solutions tailored to diverse customer needs.

Strong Supplier Relationships:
We have established long-term partnerships with leading global manufacturers, ensuring a stable supply chain, competitive pricing, and consistent quality. These alliances allow us to stay ahead of market trends and meet our clients' evolving demands.

Compliance and Sustainability:
We operate with the highest standards of compliance and ethical business practices. As we grow, we are also committed to sustainable and environmentally responsible operations, exploring eco-friendly alternatives and efficient resource management.

Where We Stand Today:
We are proud to be recognized as one of the largest importers and suppliers of PVC resin in India. Our reputation is built on unwavering quality, operational consistency, and enduring client relationships that span decades.

Looking Forward:
With a strong foundation, experienced leadership, and a clear growth strategy, we are poised to seize new opportunities, diversify our offerings, and continue delivering value to our partners, customers, and the broader industry.
