Plasticizers

Enhancing flexibility, durability, and processability in plastics and elastomers

Dioctyl Phthalate (DOP)

DOP is a widely used phthalate plasticizer that imparts flexibility and workability to PVC and other
polymers. It improves softness, elongation, and durability in wire coatings, flooring, synthetic leather,
and flexible PVC films. DOP offers excellent compatibility, stability, and low volatilityâ€”making it a
preferred choice in cost-sensitive applications.

>
Dioctyl Terephthalate (DOTP)

DOTP is a non-phthalate plasticizer increasingly used as an alternative to DOP in flexible PVC
applications. Known for its environmental safety, low toxicity, and superior plasticizing performance,
DOTP is ideal for use in automotive interiors, cables, flooring, and wall coverings. It offers excellent
resistance to extraction and migration.

>
Diisononyl Phthalate (DINP)

DINP is a high molecular weight phthalate used to impart flexibility in products requiring long-term
performance. It is extensively used in construction materials, automotive trims, and cables. DINP provides
good balance between volatility, flexibility, and low temperature performance.

>
Dioctyl Adipate (DOA)

DOA is a flexible, low-temperature plasticizer that enhances cold resistance and elasticity in flexible
PVC, polyurethane, and other synthetic rubbers. Its clarity and superior weathering make it ideal for food
packaging films, toys, and outdoor applications. DOA is also FDA-approved for food contact use.

>
Hexamoll DINCH

DINCH is a non-phthalate plasticizer designed for applications involving close human contact, such as
medical devices, toys, and food packaging. It offers excellent safety, minimal migration, and good
processing behavior. DINCH complies with global safety standards and is a preferred choice for sensitive
applications.

>
Di(2-ethylhexyl) Cyclohexane-1,2-dicarboxylate (DEHCH)

DEHCH is a non-phthalate plasticizer with low volatility and high permanence. It is used in medical
tubing, flooring, and high-end flexible PVC products. DEHCH provides excellent plasticizing performance,
clarity, and temperature resistance, while maintaining health and environmental safety standards.
