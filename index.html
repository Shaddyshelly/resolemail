About Us

Who We Are

We, Resol Vinyls and Chlorides Ltd. (RVCL) are your reliable partner for high-quality polymer products distribution.

RVCL was established in 2005 by Mr. Krishan Kumar Bansal as Salasar Impex Pvt. Ltd. Our company has undergone significant growth, transitioning to a Limited company in June 2018 and officially becoming Resol Vinyls and Chlorides Ltd. (RVCL) in March 2022.

Our leadership team — Mr. Krishan Kumar Bansal, Mr. Parth Dodeja (joined in 2016), and Mr. Vijay Rawal (joined in 2021, associated since 2019) — brings a shared commitment to growth, innovation, and customer success.

RVCL is a premier importer and distributor of polymers including PVC Resin, Paste Resin, Melamine, Citric Acid, Polyethylene, Plasticizers, and more. Our strong foundation of integrity and commitment to customers has made us a trusted partner across industries by consistently delivering value and service.

As first-generation entrepreneurs, our founders bring a forward-thinking mindset and fresh energy that continues to drive RVCL's progress.
